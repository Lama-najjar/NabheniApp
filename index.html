Born from Resilience & Innovation

Nabheni was created by Lama and Yara, two brilliant sisters from Gaza, as part of the Technovation global tech entrepreneurship competition for girls.

Despite facing incredible challenges, including displacement from their home and living in temporary shelters during conflict, they never gave up on their vision to help the deaf and hard-of-hearing community.

Their personal experiences in a conflict zone inspired them to include specialized detection for gunfire, bombing, and military aircraft - features that could save lives in dangerous situations.

Nabheni has been internationally recognized, including at the prestigious WSIS (World Summit on the Information Society) awards.
